Artisan Bread

It begins in the middle of the night when our bakers mix the doughs from scratch, and doesn’t end until the early hours of the following day. Our bakers take great pride in producing world-class bread for you to enjoy hot and fresh every day.


Hearth Breads

*Call to special order a loaf without egg wash if you have egg allergies

Baguette – Baked Fresh Every Day

The hallmark of all bakers. We take great pride in our classic baguette. Best served fresh, but if you have day old baguettes, we suggest making crostini!

Ingredients: Unbleached Wheat Flour, Water, Salt, Yeast


Spinach Bread

This is a delicious bread to serve with roasted meats, poultry or vegetables. Baked on Fridays.

Ingredients: Unbleached Wheat Flour, Water, Spinach, Olive Oil, Salt, Yeast, Garlic, Nutmeg, and Egg Wash.


Pain de Campagne

We use the same dough as our baguette to make this big round loaf. It keeps longer and is the quintessential “every day” bread. Baked everyday.

Ingredients: Unbleached Wheat Flour, Water, Salt, Yeast


Germ Levain

We use a special flour from Great River Organic Milling to make this bread – it’s got great wheat flavor and has an incredibly moist crumb. Eat with a soft, rich cheese, and it keeps for up to a week! Baked on Thursdays, Fridays and Saturdays.

Ingredients: Partially Sifted Whole Wheat Flour, Water, Whole Wheat Levain, Unbleached Wheat Flour, Salt


Green Olive

This is a true olive loaf – rich with olive oil and packed with juicy Sicilian olives. Best served with a simple pasta dinner antipasti platter. Baked on Thursdays and Saturdays.

Ingredients: Unbleached Wheat Flour, Whole Wheat Flour, Green Olives, Semolina, Water, Olive Oil, Salt, Yeast, Malt Powder; brushed with Egg before baking


Rosemary Olive Levain

We add lots of rosemary and kalamata olives to our Germ Levain dough to make this loaf. A perfect addition to any cheese platter and a superb choice for a grilled cheese sandwich. Baked on Thursdays, Fridays and Saturdays.

Ingredients: Partially Sifted Whole Wheat Flour, Water, Whole Wheat Levain, Unbleached Wheat Flour, Salt


Sourdough

One of our most popular breads! This crusty loaf has a pleasingly complex flavor from the small amount of rye and whole wheat flour. Slice thin and serve with cheese; slice thick and serve to with stews and braised meats. Baked everyday.

Ingredients: Unbleached Wheat Flour, Water, Whole Wheat Levain, Rye, Salt, Yeast


Sourdough Rye

This is a very mild rye bread with classic caraway flavor. If you like pastrami or corned beef, you’ll definitely want this bread. Baked on Thursdays.

Ingredients: Unbleached Wheat Flour, Water, Rye Flour, Whole Wheat Levain, Salt, Caraway Seeds


Stirato

Our version of ciabatta, this bread is chewy and goes with everything. Slice thick pieces, brush with olive oil, and grill for a chewy and crunchy texture. Baked everyday.

Ingredients: Unbleached Wheat Flour, Water, Salt, Yeast


Sunflower Rye Loaf and Baguette

A well-balanced rye bread chock full of toasted sunflower seeds. It’s great with a well-aged white cheddar. Baked everyday except Mondays.

Ingredients: Unbleached Wheat Flour, Water, Rye Flour, Sunflower Seeds, Molasses, Whole Wheat Levain, Oil, Salt, Yeast

Brioche

A rich, buttery pan loaf. Makes great grilled sandwiches and amazing french toast. Baked on Saturdays.

Ingredients: Unbleached Wheat Flour, Eggs, Butter, Whole Milk, Sugar, Salt, Yeast


Cardamom Bread

This is a sweet Scandinavian specialty – so of course it goes great with coffee. This lovely loaf makes a nice gift, and is always appreciated at brunch. Baked on Weekends.

Ingredients: Unbleached Wheat Flour, Whole Milk, Eggs, Sugar, Butter, Raisins, Salt, Yeast, Cardamom; topped with Brown Sugar and Almonds


Challah

The classic loaf from the Jewish tradition, most often served on Fridays. Ours comes plain, or topped with sesame or poppy seeds. Baked on Fridays.

Ingredients: Unbleached Wheat Flour, Water, Eggs, Sugar, Egg Yolks, Canola Oil, Salt, Yeast; may be topped with Sesame or Poppy Seeds


Chocolate Bread

This Italian bread is studded with chocolate chips and sweet enough to enjoy at any time of day. Pairs well with mascarpone and raspberry jam. Baked on Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays.

Ingredients: Unbleached Wheat Flour, Water, Chocolate Chips, Sugar, Cocoa Powder, Butter, Eggs, Salt, Yeast


Irish Soda Bread

This bread is hugely popular. It goes with just about anything; savory or sweet. For St. Patrick’s Day we offer a version with caraway and currants. Baked Thursday through Sunday.

Ingredients: Buttermilk, Oats, Rye Flour, All Purpose Flour, Baking Soda, Salt, Brown Sugar

Multi Grain

This loaf is packed with nine grains and sprouted wheat berries. It’s nutty-tasting and moist, not dry and saw-dusty. Baked Sundays, Tuesdays and Thursdays.

Sicilian

Ingredients: Unbleached Wheat Flour, Semolina, Water, Levain, Olive Oil,Salt, Yeast; covered with Sesame Seeds


Slicing

Honey Whole Wheat
